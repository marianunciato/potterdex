The first book in the Harry Potter series, "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone," is a worldwide success and has been translated into over 68 languages. The book has captivated millions of readers around the world with the story of a young wizard who discovers his true destiny in a magical world full of dangers and mysteries.

In addition, the cover art of the first edition of Harry Potter has been adapted in various countries, with each version featuring distinct elements of the local culture. In many different countries, the cover of the first Harry Potter book has been redesigned to reflect the cultural identity of that region, showing how the work transcended borders and became a global reference.

Through translation and cover adaptation, the Harry Potter series has become a true expression of the cultural and linguistic diversity of our world, winning fans all over the planet.
